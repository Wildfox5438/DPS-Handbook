Weapon Regulations

All on-duty officers must use approved firearms only. Firearms are issued after proper training or after your first Ride-Along (R/A). Use of non-approved firearms requires approval from Major+ or Division Director.

Firearm Authorization

  • Issuance: Firearms provided after training or first R/A.
  • Approval for Non-Issued Weapons: Commander+ or Division Director approval required.
  • Division Directors: Must seek firearm approvals from Major+.
