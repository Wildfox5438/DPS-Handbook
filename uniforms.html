Uniform Regulations

Uniforms

  • DPS Class A (Under working)
  • DPS Class B (Under working)
  • DPS Class C
  • Formal (Major+ only)

Allowed Hats

  • Police Baseball Caps (Black only)
  • Police Beanies (Black only)
  • No Hat (hair or bald)

Allowed Eyewear

  • Shades
  • Eye Glasses
  • No rainbow lenses or unprofessional styles

Duty Belts

You may use the duty belt found in the lockers.

Plate Carriers / Body Armor

Black or Dark Blue in color

Bodycams

Allowed with command approval.

Failure to comply with these regulations will result in infractions.
