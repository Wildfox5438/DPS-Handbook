CHAPTER 12. PUNISHMENTS

  • CAPITAL FELONY
    • life;
    • life without parole; or
    • death penalty
  • FIRST DEGREE FELONY PUNISHMENT
    • life or for any term of not more than 99 years or less than 5 years; and
    • a fine not to exceed $10,000
  • SECOND DEGREE FELONY PUNISHMENT
    • any term of not more than 20 years or less than 2 years; and
    • a fine not to exceed $10,000
  • THIRD DEGREE FELONY PUNISHMENT
    • any term of not more than 10 years or less than 2 years; and
    • a fine not to exceed $10,000
  • STATE JAIL FELONY PUNISHMENT
    • any term of not more than two years or less than 180 days; and
    • a fine not to exceed $10,000
  • CLASS A MISDEMEANOR
    • a fine not to exceed $4,000;
    • confinement in jail for a term not to exceed one year; or
    • both such fine and confinement
  • CLASS B MISDEMEANOR
    • a fine not to exceed $2,000;
    • confinement in jail for a term not to exceed 180 days; or
    • both such fine and confinement
  • CLASS C MISDEMEANOR
    • a fine not to exceed $500
