Welcome to the Department of Public Safety

This channel has been created as your primary resource for everything required to perform your duties as a Trooper within the Department of Public Safety. Inside, you will find the complete set of operational procedures, department regulations, communication protocols, and standards of professionalism that guide the way we function on a daily basis. These documents are designed to give you a clear understanding of expectations, responsibilities, and the methods by which we maintain order and discipline across the department.

Every section has been written to support you in carrying out your role effectively, whether you are patrolling, responding to incidents, or representing the department in any capacity. It is essential that you carefully review and familiarize yourself with all of the material provided here. Nothing in this space is optional—these are the rules, guidelines, and structures that hold our department together and ensure consistency across all operations.

Failure to comply with these procedures or a lack of knowledge regarding the information in this channel will not be excused and may result in disciplinary action. Take the time to read everything thoroughly and revisit it whenever you need clarification. Your ability to perform successfully as a Trooper depends directly on how well you understand and apply the standards set forth here.
